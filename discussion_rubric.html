Discussion & Reflection Rubric

Level 3 – Mastery

  • Addresses the prompt thoughtfully and in depth
  • Makes clear connections between ideas, history, and computing
  • Uses at least one cited source
  • Writing is clear, organized, and reflective
  • Responds meaningfully to at least one peer

Level 2 – Progressing

  • Addresses the prompt but with limited depth
  • Some connections are present but underdeveloped
  • Sources may be weak or missing
  • Peer interaction is minimal or superficial

Level 1 – Developing

  • Response is brief or partially off-topic
  • Limited evidence of reflection
  • No sources cited

Level 0 – No submission

No work submitted.
