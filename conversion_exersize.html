Decimal to Binary Conversions

1. Convert the decimal number 25 to binary.

Divide 25 by 2 repeatedly and note the remainders: 25 ÷ 2 = 12 R1, 12 ÷ 2 = 6 R0, 6 ÷ 2 = 3 R0, 3 ÷ 2 = 1 R1, 1 ÷ 2 = 0 R1. Read the remainders from bottom to top: 11001.

2. Convert the decimal number 37 to binary.

Divide 37 by 2 repeatedly: 37 ÷ 2 = 18 R1, 18 ÷ 2 = 9 R0, 9 ÷ 2 = 4 R1, 4 ÷ 2 = 2 R0, 2 ÷ 2 = 1 R0, 1 ÷ 2 = 0 R1. Read remainders from bottom to top: 100101.

3. Convert the decimal number 128 to binary.

128 is 2^7, so it's 1 followed by 7 zeros: 10000000.

4. Convert the decimal number 63 to binary.

63 is 2^6 - 1, which means it's six 1's in binary: 111111.

5. Convert the decimal number 19 to binary.

Divide 19 by 2: 19 ÷ 2 = 9 R1, 9 ÷ 2 = 4 R1, 4 ÷ 2 = 2 R0, 2 ÷ 2 = 1 R0, 1 ÷ 2 = 0 R1. Read remainders from bottom to top: 10011.

Binary to Decimal Conversions

6. Convert the binary number 10101 to decimal.

10101 = 1×16 + 0×8 + 1×4 + 0×2 + 1×1 = 16 + 0 + 4 + 0 + 1 = 21.

7. Convert the binary number 110110 to decimal.

110110 = 1×32 + 1×16 + 0×8 + 1×4 + 1×2 + 0×1 = 32 + 16 + 0 + 4 + 2 + 0 = 54.

8. Convert the binary number 11111111 to decimal.

Eight 1's in binary equals 2^8 - 1 = 256 - 1 = 255.

9. Convert the binary number 1001001 to decimal.

1001001 = 1×64 + 0×32 + 0×16 + 1×8 + 0×4 + 0×2 + 1×1 = 64 + 0 + 0 + 8 + 0 + 0 + 1 = 73.

10. Convert the binary number 10110 to decimal.

10110 = 1×16 + 0×8 + 1×4 + 1×2 + 0×1 = 16 + 0 + 4 + 2 + 0 = 22.

Decimal to Hexadecimal Conversions

11. Convert the decimal number 42 to hexadecimal.

42 ÷ 16 = 2 remainder 10. In hexadecimal, 10 is represented as A. So 42 = 2A in hex.

12. Convert the decimal number 255 to hexadecimal.

255 ÷ 16 = 15 remainder 15. In hexadecimal, 15 is F. So 255 = FF in hex.

13. Convert the decimal number 100 to hexadecimal.

100 ÷ 16 = 6 remainder 4. So 100 = 64 in hex.

14. Convert the decimal number 175 to hexadecimal.

175 ÷ 16 = 10 remainder 15. In hex, 10 is A and 15 is F. So 175 = AF in hex.

15. Convert the decimal number 31 to hexadecimal.

31 ÷ 16 = 1 remainder 15. In hex, 15 is F. So 31 = 1F in hex.

Hexadecimal to Decimal Conversions

16. Convert the hexadecimal number 2B to decimal.

2B = 2×16 + 11×1 = 32 + 11 = 43.

17. Convert the hexadecimal number FF to decimal.

FF = 15×16 + 15×1 = 240 + 15 = 255.

18. Convert the hexadecimal number A5 to decimal.

A5 = 10×16 + 5×1 = 160 + 5 = 165.

19. Convert the hexadecimal number 3E to decimal.

3E = 3×16 + 14×1 = 48 + 14 = 62.

20. Convert the hexadecimal number C8 to decimal.

C8 = 12×16 + 8×1 = 192 + 8 = 200.
